Without Gateway

Most companies run models internally but have no governance layer. The models work fine — but there's no visibility or control.
✗ No audit trail
Nobody knows who asked what, or which model answered. If something goes wrong, there's no record.
✗ No content safety
Models can return PII (credit cards, SSNs) or toxic content. Nothing catches it.
✗ No model verification
Can't prove which model version produced an answer. Was it the approved model or something else?
✗ No tamper detection
Conversations could be altered after the fact. No way to verify integrity.
✗ No usage controls
No per-team or per-user limits. No way to track who's consuming resources.
✗ No compliance evidence
When auditors ask "how do you govern your AI?" — there's nothing to show.
